FAQ

Does ListyAI post AI images?
No. You upload your real listing photos. ListyAI only helps write captions and hashtags — no AI image generation, and no “AI” labels.
Can I edit captions?
Yes — captions are editable per platform, with copy and regenerate actions.
Is the MLS import real?
In this single-file demo, it simulates an import with realistic mock property data after a short loading state.
Do you support scheduling?
Yes — scheduling adds items to your Scheduled Posts panel, with status filters and delete controls.
